hidden in plain sight.

This website is the interface for an interactive art experience which examines value and perception. I conceived, designed, and developed this project in late 2020 as a response to Stephen Hendee's project prompt "Camouflage" for MICA Forum 1.
I created this interactive art experience to help users define and materialize their relationships with objects, entities, places, and times they find personally significant. As you explore this website, I implore you to reflect on the ways context informs, and perhaps even singlehandedly creates, our individual experiences of beauty and our perceptions of art objects and beyond.
